What is the Impact of AirBnB in San Francisco?

In a lab assignment for my Critical GIS class, we used data from InsideAirBnB.com to explore the potential impacts of AirBnB in San Francisco. Some of the analyses suggest that AirBnB is taking a portion of housing off the market and that people use the website to run hotel business. To make this map, I used a combination of ESRI ArcGIS and Adobe Illustrator. This poster is designed for educational purposes for my Critical GIS class.
